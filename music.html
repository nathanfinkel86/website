Glia (recent work)

Glia is a multiplayer visual programming environment for collaborative music creation (built on CMajor, JUCE, and Yjs CRDTs). I'm an early adopter—took it on the road for successful UK shows in December 2025, stress-testing the platform in live performance contexts.

Live integration

VST3 in Ableton Live

Custom audio graphs responding to MIDI controllers and audio input. Runs alongside hardware synths, guitar, and field recordings. Designed for live reliability and fast troubleshooting.

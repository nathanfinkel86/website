Bio

Brighton-based musician and technologist.

I'm a Brighton-based musician and technologist whose work has evolved through more than twenty years of playing guitar, bass, drums, and synths within approachable analogue/digital systems.

I spend as much time shaping space for others as I do performing. I run house band rehearsals, keep jam sessions moving, and help design community-led music initiatives that welcome different abilities and comfort levels.

Since 2020 I've worked as Senior Technical Producer for Splinter Faction, delivering hybrid events for partners such as Secret Cinema and Phillips.

I collaborate on GLIA, a neural-network instrument environment, and I've been learning from Gnaoua musicians in Essaouira, Morocco, where my Moroccan Jewish ancestry draws me.

Morocco

Having travelled around Morocco and connecting with my Moroccan Jewish ancestry, I'm learning Gnaoua music in Essaouira and exploring connections between Jewish and Gnaoua traditions.

I am hoping to record a cross-over project in Essaouira with Moroccan musicans I met in 2025, who I have played with extensively in Essaouira.

I will be hoping to receive support from UK-based artistic and philanthropic organisations that are interested in cross-cultural and inter-faith collaboration, and will be posting more about this project shortly

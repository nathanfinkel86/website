Morocco recording (Essaouira)

Recording direction connected to Essaouira, blending Gnawa rhythms with electronic sound design.

The project

This is ongoing playing and collaboration with Gnawa musicians in Essaouira, building toward a recorded project. It's about exploring the rhythmic foundation of Gnawa music and finding ways to layer electronic textures without losing the core.

The intent is interfaith and cross-cultural—exploring Jewish ancestry ties and musical connection through making, not branding.

Approach

Collaboration

Working directly with Gnawa musicians—listening first, building trust, and developing ideas together.

Electronic layering

Adding electronic sound design that complements the rhythmic foundation without overwhelming it.

Cultural thread

Exploring connection through practice—playing, recording, and sharing—rather than through packaging or branding.

Status

The project is in development. I'm spending time in Essaouira, building relationships, and figuring out what the recorded work should be. Updates as things develop.

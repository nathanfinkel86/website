Ambient / electronic performance

Immersive ambient/electronic sets built around layered textures, found sound, and melodic guitar. Often improvised and shaped to the room.

What it sounds like

Textures

Layered sound, slow movement

Ambient beds with evolving harmonics, field/found sound, and live guitar lines.

Approach

Improvisation-first

Sets are responsive: adjusting density, dynamics, and pacing to the space and audience.

Format options

Live set

Ideal for listening rooms, small venues, galleries, and late-night spaces.

Collaborative

With musicians/dancers/visuals where appropriate.

Sensory-friendly

Low-glare, lower SPL, clear cues, and accessible pacing on request.

Tech notes

Performance uses a hybrid analogue/digital rig (Ableton Live + hardware synths/effects), designed for reliable routing and fast setup.

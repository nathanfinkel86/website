The format

Live set

Hybrid analogue/digital approach, designed for intimate venues and community events.

Improvised

Sets are shaped to the room, audience energy, and context—never quite the same twice.

Collaborative

Can expand into structured jams or audience-inclusive moments depending on context.

The live rig

A hybrid analogue/digital setup designed for reliable routing, fast setup, and live flexibility (including optional Glia + visuals).

Tools

Ableton + hardware

Ableton Live plus hardware synths/effects (and room to grow into visuals over time).

Streaming

Multi-cam (as needed)

Past experience with OBS/Streamlabs; can bring it back when it makes sense.

Modular

Adaptable to venue

Can scale from solo laptop setup to full rig with hardware, depending on what the venue provides.

Sound & approach

The music blends field recordings, granular textures, and melodic guitar with evolving synth beds. It's designed to create space—for listeners to settle in, and for performers (when collaborative) to find their voice without pressure.

I'm interested in atmospheres that hold attention without demanding it, and in formats that invite participation without requiring technical skill or confidence.

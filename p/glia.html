Glia (collaboration)

Glia is a neural-network instrument environment that runs as a VST in a DAW. I integrate it with a live rig for real-time MIDI/audio interaction, and took it on the road for successful UK shows in December 2025.

What I’m doing with it

Live integration

Real-time MIDI/audio

Groove Engine responds to audio input; Harmony Engine modulates/arpeggiates MIDI input from keys/synth.

Live rig Ambient / electronic

Projects

Music

Synthesiser and guitar based, situation-led music built around layered textures, found sound, and melodic guitar, mixed with the convulsions of various synthesisers. I aim to help people find rest and connection through immersive sets, almost always improvised and shaped to the space. Each session is designed to be sensory-friendly and a space that's welcome for any emotion. I also collaborate on Synths & Stories—pairing atmospheric ambient soundscapes with spoken storytelling, where the music follows the emotional arc of parables, Rumi poetry, and personal narratives.

Glia — neural network instrument

A neural-network instrument environment for generative sound design and real-time musical collaboration. I've been partnering with GLIA since 2024 to integrate real-time AI rhythm and harmony engines into my live rig while developing VDMX visual mapping and projection workflows. Took the system on the road for successful UK shows in December 2025.

What it is

Generative audio instrument

Neural-network based system with rhythm and harmony engines that respond to MIDI and audio input, generating musical decisions in real-time. Runs as VST3/AU, standalone, or web client.

How I use it

Live performance integration

Custom workflows integrated into Ableton Live, responding to MIDI controllers and audio input from hybrid synth rig. Real-world stress-testing and feedback on system stability and creative potential.

Morocco — Essaouira projects

Morocco is part of my personal story—my family is Moroccan Jewish, and I feel especially drawn to Essaouira. I've attended Berklee College of Music's summer programme there and have built long-term friendships with local musicians. Whenever I'm there I play Gnaoua music, learning directly from the masters and tracing the connections between Jewish and Gnaoua traditions.

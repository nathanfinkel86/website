Ambient / electronic performance

Sets designed for rest and presence. Space that invites you to settle in and listen. Layered textures, found sound, melodic guitar. Improvised to the room's energy.

Listen / watch Book a show

The format

Live set

Hybrid analogue/digital. Designed for intimate spaces where people can rest and settle in.

Improvised in real-time

Responsive to the room, audience, and moment. The atmosphere guides what happens next.

Can be participatory

Optional structured jams or audience moments, depending on what feels right for the space.

The live rig

A hybrid analogue/digital setup designed for reliable routing, fast setup, and live flexibility (including optional Glia + visuals).

Tools

Ableton + hardware

Ableton Live plus hardware synths/effects (and room to grow into visuals over time).

Streaming

Multi-cam (as needed)

Past experience with OBS/Streamlabs; can bring it back when it makes sense.

Modular

Adaptable to venue

Can scale from solo laptop setup to full rig with hardware, depending on what the venue provides.

Sound & approach

Field recordings, granular textures, and melodic guitar layer into evolving synth beds. The goal is simple: create space where listeners can rest and presence settles in naturally.

Whether you're listening or participating, there's no pressure. Just atmosphere that holds your attention without demanding anything from you. Music that makes room for whoever shows up.

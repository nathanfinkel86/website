GLIA

I collaborate on GLIA, a neural network instrument that generates rhythm and harmony in real time during live performance.

I've been working with GLIA since 2023, integrating its rhythm and harmony engines into my live setup. It's a neural network system that responds to MIDI and audio input from my hybrid synth rig, generating musical decisions in real time.

I run it through Ableton Live with custom workflows, and it responds to both MIDI controllers and audio input. The system creates audio-reactive interactions, so what I play shapes what it generates, and vice versa. We recently took it on the road for UK shows in December 2025.

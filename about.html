About

Background

Nathan Finkel is a Brighton-based musician and music technologist working across live performance, facilitation, and technical production. He plays guitar, bass, drums and synths, and makes immersive ambient/electronic sets using a custom hybrid analogue/digital rig—combining found sound, layered textures and melodic guitar.

He's been part of Limmud UK for 20 years. For 15 years as House Band guitarist, he's performed high-pressure, professional-level shows with visiting artists—multi-genre material, minimal rehearsal, fast learning, and band leader/liaison responsibilities. He also draws on these skills to lead workshops and facilitation work.

He's managed Jam Camp—a 50-person camp at Borderland (Sweden)—for several years, handling camp leadership, pastoral care, and inclusive jams. In 2025, as Musical Director & Curator, he coordinated a large-scale improvised performance for the main burn with minimal notice, supporting first-time performers. Multi-year commitment with "many years of graft."
